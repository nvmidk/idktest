About Wonder Egg Priority

Wonder Egg Priority is a Japanese anime series that follows the story of Ai Ohto, a high school girl who is grappling with the suicide of her best friend. One day, she is given a mysterious egg that transports her to a strange and fantastical world where she is tasked with saving the souls of young girls who have died. Along with her three other egg companions, Neiru, Rika, and Momoe, Ai faces a series of challenges and battles against monsters known as "Seeno Evils" in order to save these girls and uncover the truth behind their deaths. As the series progresses, the girls also confront their own personal demons and traumas, leading to a poignant exploration of mental health and grief.
